A Builder You Can Trust

Montagem is a different kind of architecture practice. Founded by Mayiga Joseph in 1991, we're an employee-owned firm pursuing a democratic design process that values everyone's input.

Montagem was founded on the idea that building your home should be a good experience and one that leaves you satisfied, not just with the finished work, but with the process itself.

As a company, we pride ourselves on making your project our project and never being satisifed until our high standards are met. We work with only the most professional craftsman and construction masons in the business.
